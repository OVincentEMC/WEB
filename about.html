About Me

Hi, I’m Orlie Vincent F. Gendraule! I’m currently studying Entertainment and Multimedia Computing at CIIT, with the aspiration of becoming a game developer. I’m passionate about technology, gaming, and the creativity involved in crafting fun, immersive experiences.


At the moment, I’m learning programming languages such as C++, Java, and Python. I’ve also begun exploring game engines like Unity and Unreal Engine. It’s a lot to take in, but I’m excited about the journey ahead.


Although I haven’t participated in any hackathons or game jams yet, I would love to try them someday to collaborate and learn with others.


When I’m not studying, I usually spend my time playing games, watching and reading sci-fi, or geeking out over new tech. I don’t go outside much, but I think it would be nice to spend more time outdoors—it’s something I’d like to do more often to recharge and find inspiration.


If you’re into games, tech, or just want to chat, feel free to reach out!
